# ?? ACA Project Features

## 1. Discovery (Scout Agent)
- **Power Search:** Custom Role, Domain, and Location inputs via UI.
- **Deep Scan:** Optional Playwright-based Google scraping.
- **RSS Integration:** Automated fetching from WeWorkRemotely, Remotive, etc.
- **URL Guessing:** Direct probing of 50+ top tech company boards (Greenhouse/Lever).

## 2. Intelligence (Asset Factory)
- **AI Persona:** Choose between Professional, Startup, or Technical writing styles.
- **JD Scraper:** Automatically visits the job URL to extract requirements for the AI.
- **Tailored Assets:** Generates custom PDF Resume and Cover Letter per application.

## 3. Automation (Form Filler)
- **Auto-Fill:** Launches Playwright to pre-fill standard application fields.
- **Human-in-the-Loop:** Keeps the browser open for final review and custom questions.

## 4. Management & Tracking
- **Resume Upload:** Manage your master PDF directly from the dashboard.
- **Application Log:** Every application is automatically saved to 'Applications_Log.csv'.
- **Notifications:** Desktop popups when new jobs are found.
- **Queue Filtering:** Search through your pending jobs in real-time.
